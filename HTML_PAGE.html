The Rules

Bernie Sanders' support for the Democratic presidential nomination, according to a “social media monitoring” analysis by ScottRasmussen.com at 11:59:59 p.m. (ET) on July 17, 2019, as rounded to the nearest tenth of a percentage point, shall be ranked, among all candidates listed in such series, according to the description in the contract. For purposes of this market, Sanders will be considered to be ranked above any candidates with which his score is tied at such time, as rounded to the nearest tenth of a percentage point. Should such analysis no longer include Mr. Sanders upon expiration, the contract identifying the range "5th or lower" will resolve as Yes and all others will resolve as No.

The official approval rate will be drawn from the chart titled “Today’s Support Breakdown," available upon launch of this market at https://scottrasmussen.com/2020-democratic-candidate-social-media-analysis. Should that source be unavailable for the day in question at that time, PredictIt will, at its sole discretion, continue to await its availability, select the most recent data point in the identified series or an alternate source, or construct its own polling average.

PredictIt’s decisions and determinations under this rule shall be at PredictIt’s sole discretion and shall be final.
